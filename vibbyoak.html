The solution

is the new generation of wrist and neck-worn automatic fall detector, featuring a fall trigger, replacing conventional push buttons. The enhanced reliability and the new design allow to protect frail people of any age (Epileptics, Parkinson…). The Vibby OAK features a BluetoothTM function, for a simpler configuration and a more extensive connectivity.

Functions
Manual alarm

At any time, the alarm can also be triggered by a simple pressure on the push button.

Automatic alarm

If the wearer stands up 20s after the fall detection, Vibby OAK cancels the alarm.

If the wearer stays on the floor, Vibby OAK vibrates and the LED flashes during 20s before sending an alarm
